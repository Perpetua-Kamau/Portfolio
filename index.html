A little about me

I am Perpetua Kamau, a data analyst who enjoys turning raw data into clear, actionable insights. My passion for analysis began in finance, where I discovered that behind every spreadsheet is a story waiting to be uncovered. Today, I focus on exploring data, identifying trends, solving business problems, and creating dashboards that help people make informed decisions. Excel is where I do some of my best work—from cleaning and transforming data with Power Query to building dynamic dashboards, PivotTables, advanced formulas, and data models that simplify complex information. I enjoy asking the right questions, uncovering patterns, and presenting insights in ways that are easy to understand and act upon. I'm naturally curious and open-minded, which makes working with different datasets exciting rather than intimidating. Whether it's finance, sales, operations, customer data, or any other business domain, I enjoy diving into the numbers, understanding the story they tell, and transforming them into insights that drive better decisions. .

My experience spans **finance, banking, sales, and research**, where I've analyzed data to uncover trends, improve reporting, and support better decision-making. I primarily work with **Microsoft Excel**, leveraging advanced formulas, PivotTables, Power Query, data modeling, charts, and interactive dashboards to transform raw data into actionable insights. What I enjoy most is working with data that solves real business problems. I'm comfortable exploring unfamiliar datasets and industries, asking the right questions, and adapting my analytical approach to deliver meaningful results. Every dataset presents a new story, and that's what keeps me curious.
